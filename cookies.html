Cookie Policy – NRTH
Effective Date: 16 June

This Cookie Policy explains how NRTH uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience when you visit our website or use our services.

1. What Are Cookies
• Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website.
• They help us understand user behavior, improve functionality, and personalize content.

2. Types of Cookies We Use
• Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of our website (e.g., login, navigation).
• Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site.
• Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and enhance your experience.
• Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and track marketing performance.

3. How We Use Cookies
We use cookies to:
• Ensure the proper functioning of our site
• Analyze traffic and improve performance
• Customize your experience
• Deliver targeted advertisements (where applicable)

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences
You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
• Block or delete cookies
• Set preferences for specific websites
Please note that disabling some cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Cookies
We may allow trusted third parties to place cookies for analytics, advertising, or social media purposes. These cookies are subject to the respective third-party privacy policies.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
